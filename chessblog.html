Chess Tips

Tournament Etiquette

The person with black pieces is allowed to decide the chess timer placement
Shake hands before the start of the game
All tournaments use the "Touch Move" rule; if you touch a piece, you have to move it
If you are observing a game, do not distract the players. This includes:
  • Standing too close
  • Talking
  • Giving hints or discussing the game near the players
Do not continuously offer draws
At the end of the game, shake hands with your opponent

Opening Play

    Avoid moving the side pawns
    Develop pieces towards the center
    Avoid moving the same piece twice(unless needed)
    Try to control more space

Middle Game Play

    Use pawn breaks to gain space
    Control the center
    Attack
